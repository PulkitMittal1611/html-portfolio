Top 5 Movies according to Pulkit

My top 5 movies of all-time

3 Idiots

It is a fantastic movie about College Life and Making Dreams. Truly an eye-opener.

12th Fail

A very solid movie with fantastic underdog storyline. Teaches on how to be optimistic and progress in tough phases of life.

Chhichhore

A fun to watch movie about College Life and Challenges. Pretty good comedy and also an important lesson.

Taare Zameen Par

An inspiring movie which teaches that you must do what you love.

Lagaan

A story with Cricket as its centre point and that's my favourite sport.

MS Dhoni: The Untold Story

A Sports Biopic on MS Dhoni, a cricketer I truly admire.
